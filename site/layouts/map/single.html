How to use this map

You can pan and zoom on this map using your mouse and scroll wheel, or the plus (+) and minus (-) buttons in the bottom left corner. If you know the name of the office you're looking for use the search box in the top right corner. You can toggle the different types of offices on and off using the checkboxes in the lower right corner.
